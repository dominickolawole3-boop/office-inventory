Quick Guide

Adding an Item

  • Click + Add item in the top right
  • Enter the item name, category, and where it's stored
  • Choose Count or Slider mode
  • Click Save item — saves to the shared sheet instantly

Finding an Item

  • Type in the search bar at the top of the list
  • Search works by item name, category, or location
  • Items needing reorder appear at the top in amber

Editing an Item

  • Click anywhere on the item row to open the edit panel
  • Update any field and click Save changes
  • Click Remove to delete an item
