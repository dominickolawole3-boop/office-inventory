Quick Guide

Adding an Item

  • Click + Add item in the top right
  • Enter the item name, category, and where it's stored
  • Choose Count (qty / max / reorder) or Slider (drag to show roughly how full it is)
  • Click Save item — saves to the shared sheet instantly

Finding an Item

  • Type in the search bar at the top of the list
  • Search works by item name, category, or location
  • Items needing reorder appear at the top automatically in amber

Editing an Item

  • Click anywhere on the item's row to open the edit panel
  • Update any field — name, location, stock level, or reorder threshold
  • Click Save changes — updates for everyone immediately
  • To remove an item entirely, click Remove at the bottom of the panel
